Experience and Projects

After seven years as a web industry professional, I have a wide breadth of experience. Below are projects highlight my experience in different areas of web strategy, software implementation, and design. Also included are code samples and projects from my new work as a web developer. Each project includes a case study with details of technical requirements and how those requirements met real business needs.

Matrix Group International - Project Management and Consulting

Systems Integration - CFP.net

The Certified Financial Planner Board of Standards (CFPB) has a complex web environment. They use Sitefinity for CMS, NetFORUM for their AMS, and a host of older legacy apps to manage their certification process. My team and I made it all work together.

Read the case study or visit the live site: http://cfp.net

Custom Content Management System Implementation - militarybenefity.org

The Military Benefit Association (MBA) uses the Sitefinity CMS to power their website. They have specific workflow requirements for their members to use on their site to buy, renew, or view insurance policies. My team and I built and integrate the custom forms and workflow that lets MBA conduct business everyday.

Read the case study or visit the live site: http://militarybenefit.org

Marketing and Design - chausa.org

The Catholic Health Association (CHA) celebrated their centennial anniversary in 2015. In an effort to both mark the occasion and engage members, my team and I designed and built this site describing the history and future of CHA with numerous interactive elements.

Read the case study or visit the live site: http://chausa.org/centennial
